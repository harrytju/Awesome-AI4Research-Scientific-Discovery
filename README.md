# Awesome-AI4Research-Scientific-Discovery
Awesome-AI4Research / Scientific-Discovery is a curated collection of AI-driven tools, papers, and projects focused on advancing scientific discovery.

## Overview

This repository serves as a comprehensive resource for researchers, practitioners, and enthusiasts interested in leveraging AI to drive scientific breakthroughs. It includes:
- Summaries and key insights from impactful research papers.
- Open-source projects and code implementations.
- Tools and frameworks for AI-based scientific discovery, including **Research Agents**, **Hypothesis Generation**, **Literature Review Agents**, and **AI-assisted Paper Writing**，etc.

The collection is regularly updated with new papers, implementations, and innovative approaches in AI for scientific applications.

## Structure

- **Research Papers**: A selection of key papers on AI methodologies applied to scientific discovery, including machine learning, deep learning, reinforcement learning, and more.
- **Projects & Implementations**: Open-source AI research projects that showcase real-world applications of AI in science.
- **Tools & Frameworks**: Libraries and tools designed to assist researchers in utilizing AI for scientific applications.

## Contributing

We welcome contributions to expand and enrich this repository. If you know of any relevant papers, projects, or tools that should be included, feel free to open a pull request!

## Literature Review Agents

AI-powered agents for automating the process of literature review, enabling faster and more efficient discovery of relevant research papers and findings.

### Featured Papers & Projects:

1. **AgentRxiv**
   - **Summary**: AgentRxiv is an arXiv-style collaborative platform for autonomous research agents, facilitating multi-lab, parallel research and accelerating scientific progress.
   - **Paper**: [AgentRxiv Paper](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2503.18102)
   - **GitHub**: [AgentRxiv GitHub](https://github.com/username/AgentRxiv)

## Hypothesis Generation & Problem Discovery Agents

AI agents that autonomously generate new hypotheses, identify research problems, and propose innovative experimental methods.

### Featured Papers & Projects:

2. **DeepScientist**
   - **Summary**: DeepScientist is a fully automated AI scientist system based on Bayesian optimization, capable of autonomously designing and validating new methods, surpassing human-designed SOTA.
   - **Paper**: [DeepScientist Paper](https://arxiv.org/html/2509.26603v1)
   - **GitHub**: [DeepScientist GitHub](https://github.com/ResearAI/DeepScientist)

3. **AI-Researcher**
   - **Summary**: AI-Researcher is an end-to-end autonomous scientific discovery platform that takes user inputs and autonomously generates research ideas, designs algorithms, and writes full academic papers.
   - **Paper**: [AI-Researcher Paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.18705)
   - **GitHub**: [AI-Researcher GitHub](https://github.com/HKUDS/AI-Researcher)

## Co-scientist AI Systems

AI systems that work as collaborative co-scientists with human researchers, enhancing the research process through iterative idea generation, hypothesis testing, and feedback loops.

### Featured Papers & Projects:

6. **InternAgent**
   - **Summary**: InternAgent is a multi-agent research framework that automates the research process from "idea → method → experiment," including self-evolution and human-machine co-piloting.
   - **Paper**: [InternAgent Paper](https://arxiv.org/html/2505.16938v1)
   - **GitHub**: [InternAgent GitHub](https://github.com/Alpha-Innovator/InternAgent)

7. **Google AI Co-scientist**
   - **Summary**: Google's Gemini 2.0-based multi-agent system for scientific research that enhances hypothesis generation and experimental design in various biomedical fields.
   - **Paper**: [Google AI Co-scientist Paper](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2502.18864)
   - **Website**: [Google AI Co-scientist](https://research.google/blog/accelerating-scientific-breakthroughs-with-an-ai-co-scientist/)
